Upload Instructions

Easy Steps / 4 steps

Follow these instructions to upload and process your 3D model effectively.

  • Step 1: Register an account or log in to your existing account to start uploading models.
  • Step 2: Upload your 3D model in supported formats: .fbx, .obj, .gltf, etc.
